Our Story

We started Flexnode as two idealistic people trying to change the world.

There are enough half assed products in the world to make us do something about it. We believe that well designed products can change lives so we dedicated our time to the entire process of its creation.

That is our art.
That is what makes us craftsmen.

A craftsman has the responsibility of producing his best. Born out of respect for himself, his clients, his peers, and the art of creation itself.

Our team is made of craftsmen. We involve ourselves in every aspect of its creation. From the inception of an idea till the moment it reaches the hands of its users. And we wont stop there.

That is our pride.
That is why we love our job.

We want to start a movement. One that revives the process of product creation as a craft.

If you believe in craftsmanship, join us and together we can make the world a little better.
